## CITATION FORMAT

This textbook uses **author-date** citation format:

- **In-text**: (Author Year) or (Author Year: Page) for direct quotes
- **Multiple authors**: (Author and Author Year) for two authors; (Author et al. Year) for three or more
- **Multiple citations**: (Author Year; Author Year) or (Author Year, Author Year)

Example: "Research suggests comprehensive sanctions succeed in perhaps 20-30% of cases (Hufbauer, Schott, Elliott, and Oegg 2007)."
