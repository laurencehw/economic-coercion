## Metrics and Goals

### Word Count Targets
- Each chapter: 8,000-12,000 words
- Total manuscript: 90,000-120,000 words
- Executive summaries: 500-800 words each
- Case studies: 1,000-1,500 words each

### Visual Content Targets
- Minimum 5 figures per chapter
- 50+ total maps, charts, diagrams
- 1 tabletop exercise per chapter (10 total)
- 20-30 case studies across all chapters

### References Target
- 30-50 references per chapter
- 400-500 total unique sources
- Mix of academic, policy, practitioner sources

## Risk Management

### Potential Challenges
1. **Scope creep** - Mitigation: Stick to chapter outlines
2. **Data access issues** - Mitigation: Identify alternative sources early
3. **Time slippage** - Mitigation: Weekly progress reviews
4. **Maintaining Chinese perspective quality** - Mitigation: Consult Chinese-language sources, seek expert review

### Contingency Plans
- If behind schedule: Prioritize core chapters (1-8), simplify Chapter 9-10
- If data unavailable: Document limitation, use proxy measures
- If expert review delayed: Proceed with self-review, incorporate feedback in next edition
